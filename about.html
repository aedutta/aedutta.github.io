About Me

Hey again! My name is Ashmit and I am a senior at Langley High School.
I have been pretty involved in the physics community ever since my freshman year of high school when I co-founded the Online Physics Olympiad during COVID-19, which has grown to become the largest online physics competition. I also have spent a lot of time creating the physics curriculum and teaching at Everaise Academy, a nonprofit STEM program. I also have co-founded Physoly: an online community with over 9k members. I spend a lot of time coding, whether it be through the USA Computing Olympiad, or learning new things on my own.
In college, I want to investigate further into the scopes of quantum computation. This is an interest of mine that has grown ever since attending the Quantum School for Young Scientists organized by the University of Waterloo, and I am open to any opportunities for research.
